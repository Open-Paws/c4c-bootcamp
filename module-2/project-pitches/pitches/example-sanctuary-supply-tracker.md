# Sanctuary Supply Tracker

**Pitched by:** [Example Student]
**Category:** Animal Advocacy

## The Problem
Animal sanctuaries often operate on tight budgets and struggle to coordinate supply donations. When one sanctuary has excess hay and another is running low, there's no easy way to connect them. Supplies get wasted while nearby sanctuaries go without.

## The Tool
A simple web app where sanctuaries can list what they have surplus of and what they need. Matches are suggested based on location and urgency. Email/SMS notifications when a match is found. Dashboard showing network-wide supply levels.

## Who It Helps
Animal sanctuaries, farm animal rescue organisations, and donors who want to contribute supplies rather than cash.

## Tech I Think It Needs
Web app (maybe React), database for listings, geolocation for matching, notification system, maybe an API so other sanctuary management tools can integrate.

## Why I Care About This
I volunteered at a sanctuary that had to turn away a rescue because they couldn't afford feed that week. A sanctuary 30 minutes away had just received a bulk donation they couldn't store. That shouldn't happen.
